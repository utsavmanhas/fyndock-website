The cost of flying blind

What happens when finance
has no rhythm.

For Indian startups and MSMEs, financial opacity is not abstract. It shows up as penalties, wasted founder hours, and invisible cash leakage.

Missed compliance filing
A single missed GSTR-3B triggers late fees from day one. Incorrect filings attract 18% interest per annum on unpaid tax — accumulating silently for months.

Indian startup founders spend 12 to 18 hours per month chasing accountants, reconciling mismatches, and preparing investor reports manually.
