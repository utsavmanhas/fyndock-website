The Origin

From a CA firm founded in 1992 to the CFO layer startups always needed.

In 1992, S.P. Manhas founded a Chartered Accountancy practice in New Delhi with one belief: that good financial advice should be available to every business, not just large corporates with CFO budgets.

Thirty years later, that practice has served 1,000+ clients, built expertise across 15+ industries, and maintained a 98% client retention rate most consulting firms would envy.

Fyndock is the next chapter — what happens when you combine 30 years of CA institutional depth with VC/PE analytical training and AI-powered MIS infrastructure. The result is something the market has never had before: a CFO function that is simultaneously precise, strategic, and affordable for growth-stage companies.

"We are, frankly, the CFO your CA firm was never trained to be."
