The Process

From trial balance to
board-ready in 24 hours.

Three phases. No complex onboarding. No data migration. We work with what you have.

Phase 1
Onboarding
Week 1
Discovery call (30 mins)
We learn your business model, accounting system, and current reporting gaps. No homework required on your end.
Assessment + configuration
We assess your accounting system and configure your Fyndock MIS template for your specific industry — SaaS, D2C, EduTech, or otherwise.
No data migration needed
You keep your existing accounting software. We work with your trial balance export — Tally, Zoho, QuickBooks, or any CSV.
Phase 2
First MIS Report
Days 1–5
You export the trial balance
A standard export from Tally or Zoho Books. Takes 2 minutes. You email it or share via a secure link.
AI structures and categorises
Our AI engine maps your chart of accounts, builds the 8 sheets, selects your industry KPIs, and flags potential anomalies for CA review.
CA team validates every number
A qualified CA reviews the AI output — every figure, every reconciliation, every KPI. The AI drafts; the CA signs off.
CFO narrative written
We write the monthly CFO commentary — plain English, no jargon, with specific observations and recommended actions.
8-sheet Excel delivered + 30-min walkthrough
You receive the workbook. We walk you through it — what the numbers say, what to watch, what to act on.
Phase 3
Ongoing Cadence
Every month
Trial balance in → MIS out within 48hrs
Every month, same process. You send the TB export, we return a complete MIS workbook. No chasing. No delays.
Red flags surfaced before you discover them
We identify issues before they become problems — cash runway pressure, GST mismatches, debtor ageing creep, margin erosion.
Quarterly strategic review
Deeper review every quarter — budget vs actuals analysis, rolling forecast update, strategic priorities for the next quarter.
